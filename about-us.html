SOCIAL ACTIVITIES

At RYAN SPORTS CLUB, we are committed to bringing good values ​​to the community, improving people's quality of life through exercise training and access to health care services.

That's why we have cooperated with Vinacapital Foundation in this program Children Heartbeat Fund to support congenital heart surgery for 10,000 poor children under the age of 18 - wishing to bring a healthy heartbeat for future generations.

INNOVATION COOPERATION PROGRAM

We cooperate with many companies and academies to raise awareness of community in building a healthy lifestyle and practice habits in life. Tinifit is one of our most enthusiastic cooperation programs in recent times. This is an exclusive model combining exercise with fun activities for children developed by TiniWorld and RYAN sports club. Tinifit will go into operation at more than 20 facilities nationwide.
